SOUTH CAROLINA CRIMINAL DEFENSE & PERSONAL INJURY LAWYER

You don't have time for problems. If you need
answers, you can get help right now.

When you are accused of a crime, it is a frightening experience. If it is your first time, you do not know who to turn to for help or what to do next. However, every step that you take can have a serious impact on the outcome of your case. You need a lawyer who can help protect your legal rights and a law firm that knows what to do at every stage of the case.

For more than 24 years, the Woods Law Firm has protected the rights of people accused of a crime in South Carolina. We have experience in all 46 counties in the state and a team of experienced and skilled criminal defense attorneys who have won thousands of cases for our clients.
